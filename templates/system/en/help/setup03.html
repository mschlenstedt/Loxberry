Network Settings

Network-Type: LAN / Wired Network or WLAN / Wireless Network.
Please choose the type of physical connection to your Loxberry.

Network-Device: Please select your device to be used to establish a network link.

WLAN (SSID): Please enter the name of your WiFi / WLAN.

WLAN Password: Please enter your WiFi / WLAN Password.

Network Addresses: Please select the method to obtain an IP-Address. Either Automatic (DHCP) or Manual management (Static IP)

IP-Address: In case of Static IP management enter an IP-Address here. An IP-Address has the format xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx, e. g. 192.168.1.100

IP-Mask: In case of Static IP management enter the IP-Subnet Mask of you local network. In many cases it's 255.255.255.0

IP-Address of the Gateway:In case of Static IP management enter the IP-Address of the Gateway. In most cases the IP of your Router/Fritz!Box.

IP-Address of the Nameserver:In case of Static IP management enter the IP-Address of the Name Server (DNS). In most cases the IP of your Router/Fritz!Box.
